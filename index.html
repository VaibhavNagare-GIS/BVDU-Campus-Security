🔍 Key Insights

Critical findings from the January 2026 field survey. These observations directly inform campus security planning and infrastructure investment priorities.

⚠️
16 Open Posts — No Shelter

Dental College, JNIT, Ayurved Hospital, Homeopathic College, Nursing College, and College of Fine Arts all have guards stationed with no weather protection. 4 of these also have no seating — the worst conditions on campus.

🏠
Women's Hostel Security Gaps

3 of 5 women's hostel posts — PG Girls Hostel, Nursing College Hostel, and Girls Hostel A — are open posts with a single guard. Only New Ladies Hostel is fully covered (permanent cabin, 3+ guards, score 7/7).

✅
Main Gate Strongly Covered

The primary Front Gate scores 6/7 with 2 guards and a permanent cabin — one of only 2 high-quality posts on campus. Bharati Vidyapeeth School Gate also holds a permanent cabin post.
